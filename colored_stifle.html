Stifle joint of the dog:

This is a model of the stifle joint of the dog preserved by Elnady technique and stained to show the various ligaments of the joint. 

Notice, part of the quadriceps femoris muscle, the patella, the patllar ligament and the two femoropatellar ligaments are reflected cranioventrally   

Yellow = Collateral ligaments

orange = cranial meniscotibial ligament of medial meniscus

Purple = transverse meniscal ligament  

Red = meniscofemoral and caudal meniscal ligaments of the lateral meniscus 

Black= caudal cruciate ligament 

Green = cranial cruciate ligament

Purple = fabellae (sesamoids of gastrocnemius muscle)                                                                                                                                                                               Home
